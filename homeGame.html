Welcome to the game of ☆ Shooting Stars ☆

I am waiting for the stars to appear. Please help me to shoot the stars so my wishes may come true! ♡


HOW TO PLAY ?

  1. Click "Start Game" button to start.
  2. Move the tank using your keyboard (ARROW LEFT to go left and ARROW RIGHT to go right).
  3. Shoot the stars by pressing the SPACE bar.
  4. To continue to the next level, press the SPACE bar.
  5. Check your score. If game over, refresh the page to continue playing the game.
